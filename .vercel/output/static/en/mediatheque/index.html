The media library

A reading and discovery space for everyone

The media library is a place open to everyone, dedicated to reading, discovery and sharing. Novels, comics, documentaries, magazines and audiobooks: everyone can find something to inform themselves, escape or be entertained. Friendly and welcoming, it offers spaces to read, browse through books and enjoy a quiet moment, alone, with family or with friends.

The game library

Time to play !

Every four months, we renew our game library to offer you even more ways to have fun with family or friends. Our selection of games is suitable for all ages, from 3 years old, and appeals to both children and adults. You can play freely on site, and games can be borrowed with a media library subscription to enjoy them at home too.

The cyberbase

Your workspace unlike any other

The workspace is free and open to everyone. It offers a calm and comfortable setting to work alone or in groups, study, read or complete online tasks. Free Wi-Fi access is available, as well as computers for on-site use. This space is designed to promote concentration, exchange and autonomy, in a friendly atmosphere and an exceptional setting.

Other media formats

CDs, DVDs, vinyl and online clicks!

The media library also offers a wide selection of multimedia formats: CDs, DVDs and vinyl for all tastes. Your subscription also gives you access to a multitude of online resources, to listen, watch or discover content from home or on site. Music, films, online press, tutorials, documentaries and more: everything is at your fingertips to enrich your leisure time and discoveries.
