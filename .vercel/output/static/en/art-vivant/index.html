Artist residency

Supporting artists, close to creation

Artist residency is a privileged time dedicated to creation, research and experimentation. It offers artists and companies a space, resources and a conducive environment to develop an artistic project, refine their writing or finalize a work.

By hosting artists in residency, we affirm our commitment to creation and support for local companies. These work periods are an opportunity to accompany artists in their journey while offering the public a privileged look at the birth of a work during residency presentations.
