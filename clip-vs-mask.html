clip-path — hard geometric cut

Pixels outside the shape are fully removed. No soft edges. Best for shapes and angled cuts.

circle()

clip-path: circle(50%)

polygon() — hexagon

clip-path: polygon(25% 0%, 75% 0%, …)

polygon() — angled corner

← same as your collage photos

clip-path: polygon(0 0, 100% 0, 100% 70%, 70% 100%, 0 100%)

inset() — rounded corners

clip-path: inset(0 round 24px 24px 4px 24px)

mask — alpha/luminance based visibility

White in mask = visible. Black = invisible. Grey = semi-transparent. Use for soft edges and fades.

gradient fade bottom

mask: linear-gradient(to bottom, black 50%, transparent)

radial vignette

mask: radial-gradient(ellipse, black 40%, transparent)

diagonal soft wipe

← impossible to soft-edge with clip-path

mask: linear-gradient(135deg, black 30%, transparent 70%)

SVG shape mask

mask: url(data:image/svg+xml, …ellipse…)

combining both

clip-path for the outer polygon shape + mask for a soft fade. Used in collages like yours.

angled corner + bottom fade

clip-path: polygon(…) + mask: linear-gradient(…)
